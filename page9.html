Choose Your Own Adventure Game (CYOAG)

Mystery Adventure


Within a few minutes, you approach an old but serviceable house. It is a single-story cottage with boarded windows and a door hanging on one hinge.
Despite all that, it is clearly a better place to spend the night than the open forest.
As you stand there taking it all in, you notice an old man heading through the front door.


  • To follow him, go to 10.

  • To head to the back of the house for a better look, go to 11.
