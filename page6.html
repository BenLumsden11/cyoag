Choose Your Own Adventure Game (CYOAG)

Mystery Adventure


The old man leads you through knee-high bushes and between twisted trees that reach out and claw at your clothes, but you ignore all of the pain and discomfort to keep up with him.
For somebody so old, he is incredibly sprightly, and he regularly disappears behind distant bushes or thick tree-trunks.
Eventually, you catch up with him just as he comes to a halt in front of a wooden cottage.
It has seen better days, but it still offers some protection from whatever is lurking in the forest.
You follow the man through the front door.
