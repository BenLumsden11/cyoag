Hello and welcome to my website!

This website is a Choose Your Own Adventure Game, the storyline of this game is that you were
driving through the dark spooky forest to collect papers from your ex-husband but your car
breaks down in the middle of the forest forcing you to walk through the forest and make sensible decisions
to get you home safely.
