Choose Your Own Adventure Game (CYOAG)

Mystery Adventure


You turn around and stare up into the wrinkled face of an old man. He doesn't look friendly, as such, but nor is he immediately threatening.
If anything, he looks annoyed that he's had to rescue you. He offers you his hand to help you up, and you take it.
His skin is ice-cold and smooth to the touch, but he is surprisingly strong for somebody so small.
"Come with me," he says. "I will take you to where you need to be." Without waiting for you to answer, the man sets off along a hidden track.


  • To follow him, go to 6.

  • To turn and sprint in the other direction, go to 5.
